Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book a room?

Rooms can be booked directly from the Rooms page by selecting your preferred room type and clicking the “Book Now” button. You may also contact our reception team for assistance.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellations are free up to 24 hours before the scheduled check-in time. After that period, one night’s charge may apply as per terms.

What amenities are included in the rate?

All room rates include complimentary Wi-Fi, fitness center access, bottled water, concierge support, and daily housekeeping.

What are the difference between weekday and weekend rates?

Weekend rates (Friday and Saturday nights) are priced slightly higher due to increased demand. All other nights follow standard weekday pricing.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes. For bookings of 10 or more rooms, special discounted rates are available. Please contact our events team through the Contact page for customized quotations.

Which payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, bank transfers, and online payments. Details are available under the Pricing and Policies sections.

How can I get help while navigating the website?

Use our AI-powered concierge assistant available as a chat icon on every page. Simply type what you are looking for in natural language, and the assistant will guide you instantly to the correct page or section.
